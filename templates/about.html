About Healthcare Assistant

The Smart Healthcare Assistant is an AI-powered platform designed to provide accurate and helpful medical information. It leverages advanced natural language processing and a specialized knowledge base to offer reliable healthcare guidance.

Important Disclaimer

This AI assistant provides general health information and is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns.
